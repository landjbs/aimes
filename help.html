Help

  • How can I best take advantage of AIMES’ intelligent-classification abilities? We’re so glad you asked! AIMES currently has two classifying abilities: Quick Scan and Full Scan. To run either of these, you first need to create an account and log in. After having done so, you should see links to both Quick Scan and Full Scan in the top left corner of the screen. Simply navigate to whichever piques your interest, fill out the form, and click “Scan” to get immediate medical feedback.
    Even if you don’t have any of your medical data accessible, you can test AIMES’ functionality by plugging arbitrary numbers into our forms. The placeholders in each text box represent an average value of that variable for a healthy person, so those are always a good starting point!
  • How do I navigate back to the index or homepage?There will always be a little button named AIMES in the top left corner of the screen. Simply click that to return to the index (if you aren’t logged in) or homepage (if you are).
  • How do I log out of my account? There is a button labeled Log Out in the top right corner of all login-accessible pages. Clicking it will log you out of your account and return you to the index.
  • Can I delete my account or change my password?Not yet but stay tuned! These functionalities are coming ASAP!!
