How It Works

AIMES uses state of the art techniques for analysis and dimesionality reduction, and has trained on over 25,000 datapoints to provide users with straight-forward and comprehensive feedback. Simply upload your symptoms or medical file and recieve immediate evaluation!

Data Generation

AIMES' first goal is to find and format medical data with which to train the algorithms. Most of this data is pulled directly from thoroughly-vetted medical files, and the rest is simualted along probability distributions fine-tuned to the most recent medical consnensus. This data exists in between 11 and 20 dimensions, and is sculpted to best train our learning system.

Machine Learning

We spared no detail in the design of AIMES, carefully crafting an AI that is both intelligent and efficient. Our "Random Forest Classifier" is geared for rapid and accurate binary classification, screening for disease or unhealthy habits in just milliseconds. The algorithm creates a host of binary decisions trees as it parses through the data, with each "tree" casting a vote toward the final decision at their terminal node.
