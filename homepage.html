AIMES is founded on a simple principle: everyone should have access to intelligent medical analysis. Whether it be constructive feedback on your daily habits or a heads-up about the onset of something more serious, we're here to give you the information you need to stay healthy.

Quick Scan: Why should you have to choose between your schedule and your health? With quick scan you won't have to. In just a of couple minutes every day, you can recieve detailed feedback on your wellbeing and habits. Quick scan will help you stay healthy—so you can focus on the more important things.

Try it!

Full Scan: No matter your access to medical care, you should never have to be blindsided by news about your own health. Full scan is designed to give you comprehensive diagnostic feedback on your medical data, letting you know when your symptoms give cause for concern. Though it is currently designed to recognize kidney disease, full scan's learning system will soon be expanded to much more—keeping you protected and informed.

Try it!
